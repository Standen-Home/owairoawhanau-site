## A simple practice routine (10 minutes)
1. **Whakahā** (2–3 slow breaths)
2. **Hum** the melody (no words yet)
3. Sing **line by line** (repeat each line 3 times)
4. Put the verse together
5. Finish by singing it once through calmly

## Helpful habits
- Practise little and often.
- Record yourself (even just voice memo) and listen back.
- If there are actions, practise the **rhythm first**, then add words.
