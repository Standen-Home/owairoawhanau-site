## Vowels (oropuare)
- **a** = “ah”
- **e** = “eh”
- **i** = “ee”
- **o** = “aw”
- **u** = “oo”

## Tohutō / macrons
A macron (tohutō) usually means **hold the vowel longer** (e.g. *ā, ē, ī, ō, ū*).

## Learning tips
- Go **slow first**, then speed up.
- Clap/tap the beat to keep words together.
- If you’re unsure, ask someone to model the line — copying rhythm helps.
