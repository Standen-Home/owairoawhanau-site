## Tikanga
A few gentle guidelines for pepeha in our space:

- **Your pepeha is personal.** Share what you’re comfortable sharing.
- **Don’t claim iwi/hapū/marae links you don’t have.** If you’re still learning your connections, it’s okay to leave those lines out.
- **If you’re non‑Māori, it’s normal to anchor to where you’re from and where you live now** (whenua tupu / kāinga), and to acknowledge ancestry honestly.
- **Keep it simple and respectful.** You can always add more as you learn.

If you’re unsure, ask a kaiako/kaumātua/whānau member — local guidance is best.

## Templates
Mix and match what fits for you. It’s okay to keep it simple.

<div class="cols-2">
  <div>
    <h3>Pepeha (general)</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Tēnā koutou katoa</strong> — Hello to you all</li>
      <li><strong>Ko _ te maunga</strong> — is the mountain</li>
      <li><strong>Ko _ te awa / roto</strong> — is the river / lake</li>
      <li><strong>Ko _ te waka</strong> — is the waka</li>
      <li><strong>Ko _ te iwi</strong> — are the tribe</li>
      <li><strong>Ko _ te hapū</strong> — are the subtribe</li>
      <li><strong>Ko _ te marae</strong> — is the marae</li>
      <li><strong>Nō _ ahau</strong> — I’m from</li>
      <li><strong>Ko _ tōku ingoa</strong> — My name is</li>
      <li><strong>Tēnā tātou katoa</strong> — Greetings everyone</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div>
    <h3>Options for non‑Māori</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Tēnā koutou katoa</strong> — Hello to you all</li>
      <li><strong>Te whakapaparanga mai _</strong> — is my ancestry <em>(e.g. Ko Kōtirana — Scotland/Scottish)</em></li>
      <li><strong>Engari</strong> — but, however</li>
      <li><strong>Ko _ te whenua tupu</strong> — <em>Placename</em> is where I grew up</li>
      <li><strong>Ko _ te kāinga</strong> — <em>Place</em> is my home</li>
      <li><strong>Nō _ au</strong> — I'm from <em>place</em></li>
      <li><strong>Kei _ au e noho ana</strong> — I am living in <em>place</em></li>
      <li><strong>He _ au i _</strong> — I am <em>job title</em> at <em>workplace</em></li>
      <li><strong>Ko _ au</strong> — I am <em>Name</em></li>
      <li><strong>Tēnā tātou katoa</strong> — Greetings everyone</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
