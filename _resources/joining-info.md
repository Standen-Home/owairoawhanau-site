## Who it’s for
Whānau and community who want to learn and support waiata at Ō Wairoa Marae.

## What to bring
- A water bottle
- A warm layer
- A good attitude (that’s the main one)

## New to te reo?
All good. Come along, listen first, join in when you’re ready.
