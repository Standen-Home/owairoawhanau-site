## A simple guideline
These resources are for learning and supporting our kaupapa.

- Don’t re-upload or redistribute material that isn’t meant for public sharing.
- Credit composers/kaitito where known.
- If you’re unsure whether something should be public, keep it **whānau-only**.

If you have questions, check with the rōpū leads.
