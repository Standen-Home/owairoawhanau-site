## Kupu
Let us be staunch
in speaking Māori!
my noble language
my precious inheritance.
Astound me
when speaking Māori.
Let us be staunch
in speaking Māori!
Kia kaha tātou
Ki te
(Hī!)
kōrero Māori!
E minaka ana taku waha
Ki te kai a te rangatira,
Taku reo rangatira
Taku kuru pounamu tuku iho.
Mīharo kē ana
Ki tōna pakari kia ora
Tē memeha, te wairua
ki te kōrero Māori
Kia kaha tātou
Ki te kōrero Māori!

## Meaning
I desire that
my talk be like that of a leader,
with your maturity by
the evanescence, the spirit you show
