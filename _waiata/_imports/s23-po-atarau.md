## Kupu
<pre class="lyrics">
E moea iho nei
E haere ana
Koe ki pāmamao
Haere rā
Ka hoki mai anō
Ki te tau
E tangi atu nei
On a moonlit night
You going away
Weeping here
When we must say goodbye
Soon you'll be sailing
While your away
Pō atarau
</pre>


## Meaning
<div class="meaning">
I see in a dream
To a distant land
Farewell,
But return again
To your loved one,
Now is the hour
Far across the sea
Oh please remember me
When you return you'll find me waiting here
</div>
