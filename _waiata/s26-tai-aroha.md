## Kupu
<pre class="lyrics">
Ko te aroha anō he wai
E pupū ake ana
He awa e māpuna mai ana
I roto i te whatu-mānawa
(x2)
Ko tōna mātāpuna he hōhonu
Ā inā ia ka rere anō
(x2)
He tai timu
He tai pari
He tai ope
He tai roa
He tai nui
Na Anaru Kupenga, nō Ruatoria
continually bubbling up
from within your soul.
it has a soothing effect
an ebb tide
an incoming tide
a forceful tide
a long-lasting tide
a full tide.
