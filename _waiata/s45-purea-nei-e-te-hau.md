## Kupu

Purea nei e te hau
Horoia e te ua

## Meaning
Cleansed by the wind,
washed by the rain,
and warmed by the sun,
troubles are cleared away
and restraints are released.

Fly, free spirit, fly,
to the worlds of the heavens.
