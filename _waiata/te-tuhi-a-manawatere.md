## Meaning
<div class="meaning">
The mark of Manawatere.
Ancestor of Ngāi Tai.
Astral travel… waka a feather.
Pōhutukawa… Howick (Cockle Bay).
A proverb.
The mark left on the pōhutukawa tree.
</div>
