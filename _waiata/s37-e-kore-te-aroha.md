## Kupu
E kore te aroha e taka e
I waho i taku mānawa e

Uia e hine, he aha rā?
He aroha e

Nau mai ki te whare āhuru e
Kaua e rurutu roimata e

Uia e hine, he aha rā?
He aroha e

Kīhai i manakohia
Ngā kupu mīharo e

Ka hoki atu te tau
Me whakarongo mai rā
