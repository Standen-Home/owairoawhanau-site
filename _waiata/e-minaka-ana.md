## Meaning
<div class="meaning">
Let us be staunch
in speaking Māori.
I desire my talk
to be like that of a leader,
my noble language,
my precious inheritance.

Astound me
with your maturity by
the evanescence, the spiriti you show
when speaking Māori.

Let us be staunch
in speaking Māori.
</div>
