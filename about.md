This site supports the **rōpū waiata of Owairoa Marae** — a place to learn, practise, and share waiata in a way that’s warm, respectful, and easy for whānau to maintain.

## How our hui often runs
We follow a simple flow (karakia, kōrero, waiata, notices, closing). The full outline and templates (pepeha, etc.) are in **[Rauemi](/resources/)**.

## Tikanga / respectful use
Please use and share these resources appropriately.
